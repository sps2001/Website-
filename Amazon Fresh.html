Amazing Benefits of Amazon Audible Membership

        Is your busy schedule keeping you away from that novel on the shelf you have been wanting to read? What if we say you can catch-up on all your reading even while you are working in the kitchen or
        
        driving or even while travelling in the metro? Read ahead to know more…
       
 
       Do you spend hours commuting to your workspace or college? Are you tired of scrolling through your social media feeds throughout the travel time? Do you often doze-off while reading a book? 
       
       If you’d like to expand your knowledge or just get inspiration from books but are struggling to find the time to sit down and read, have you tried using audiobooks?

       if this sounds interesting, the next question is, have you tried Amazon Audible? Amazon Audible is a service created by Amazon which allows its users to download audiobooks and listen to them 
       
       anytime. With a selection of over 200,000 titles, there are more than enough audiobooks to suit your taste. 

      
1. Great books, Greater Collection
 
From Indian Classics to International bestsellers, there are over 200,000 audiobooks available on Audible. What more? There are many books with the author’s narration, On Writing by Stephen King is one 

such example.

There’s something intimate about having the author of a book read it to you the way he or she intends. The experience is eccentrically spell-binding.

 
2. Your pace is the book's pace

The Audible app is available on phones, tablets, desktops and is compatible with both Android as well as iOS devices. One highlight feature of the app is that you can slow down or speed up the 

play-speed of each audiobook at your convenience. For readers who want to immerse themselves in a story when listening to audiobooks, this is a welcome feature. If you are an enthusiastic 

reader of knowing the climax now and here you can listen to Audible audiobooks three times faster than normal.

 
3. Your First Audiobook is Free

If you’re unsure about your experience with this new way of reading books, you can subscribe for a 30-day free trial and get one free audio-book. If you don’t like the audiobook service, after 30 days 

you can cancel your membership and keep your free audiobook. A win-win situation for any reader. Prime members can avail 90-day free trial as against 30 days free trial for regular customers.

 
4. Refund for Every Wrong Choice You Make

Audible has a flexible policy of returning audiobooks. So if you purchased a book based on its reviews but it ended up to be a yawn-worthy purchase, Audible will refund your money. Audible has a Great 

Listens Guarantee whereby they will return any book you bought over the past 365 days if you don’t like it.

 
5. Audible is Affordable

You can either buy titles individually on Audible or pay a monthly fee of Rs. 199/- as credit. You can use this credit to buy any audiobook on Audible. Exclusive members get 30% off on any additional 

audiobook.Paying a monthly fee to get Audible membership is worth it if you listen to audio on the go and love reading.

Make the most of your time while commuting, exercising and traveling by listening to your favorite reading titles. Get your Subscription today!
